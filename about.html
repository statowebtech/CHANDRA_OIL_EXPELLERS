ABOUT US

Our company Chandra Oil Expellers is a Coconut Oil manufacturing Unit situated in Kerala, India. We were established in 1956 and have gone under various modernizations throughout the years to increase our efficiency and production capacity. There were well over 100 coconut oil facilities here in alappuzha, Kerala but only our unit has survived the test of time. We believe we owe our success over the years to our perseverance and commitment to maintain the quality of our product and keep our customers trust. Our primary sales are domestic here in Kerala but we also have a sales depot in Orissa and are doing export. We manufacture roasted coconut oil which has a golden yellow texture and a sweet aroma. They have a stronger smell and taste compared to their Virgin coconut Oil counterpart. Our oil is promoted mainly for cooking but is perfectly viable as hair oil or massage oil.
